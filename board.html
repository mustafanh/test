Design Philosophy

Living Paper represents a fusion of traditional artistic craftsmanship and modern design principles. The striking white horse on a deep red background symbolizes the dynamic spirit of creativity.

Each element carefully creates a harmonious blend of classical and contemporary artistic expression, making this not just a book, but a piece of art.

Artistic Elements

  • Dynamic composition with central white horse
  • Contrasting color scheme of deep red and gold
  • Traditional artistic tools and symbols
  • Modern typography and layout
  • Interactive design elements
